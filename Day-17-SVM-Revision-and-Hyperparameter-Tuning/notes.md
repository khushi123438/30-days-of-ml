# 📊 Key Learnings

- SVM works by finding the optimal hyperplane that maximizes the margin between classes.
- Support vectors are the most influential data points in determining the decision boundary.
- Kernel functions enable SVM to handle complex non-linear datasets.
- Hyperparameters such as C and Gamma significantly affect model performance.
- GridSearchCV helps automate the process of finding optimal parameter combinations.
- LinearSVR and SVR serve different purposes depending on dataset complexity and size.
