# 🧠 Problem Statement

A biomedical research institute, **NovaGen Research Labs**, is analyzing health records of approximately **9,800 individuals**.

The objective is to predict whether an individual belongs to the **Healthy** or **Unhealthy** category based on multiple health-related attributes.

The project aims to support:

- Clinical Trial Participant Selection
- Risk-Based Population Stratification
- Longitudinal Health Outcome Analysis
- Healthcare Research and Decision Making
